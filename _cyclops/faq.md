# Frequently Asked Questions

Below is a list of frequently asked questions.

### Why isn’t my render the same size as my composition?

Cyclops records what you see in the composition panel, so the render comes out at the size shown on screen — not at the composition’s actual dimensions. A 1920x1080 composition zoomed to 50% will render as 960x540. (High-DPI displays like Retina muddle this a little — the screen draws extra detail, so what looks like 50% zoom may capture closer to full size.)

For a render at the composition’s full dimensions, set the zoom to 100% and make the composition panel big enough that none of the comp gets cut off. Pressing the tilde key (`~`) while hovering over the panel makes it fullscreen, which usually gives plenty of room.

### Cyclops is not rendering nulls, motion paths, or layer bounding boxes inside of precomps even with Collapse Transformation on. Why?

Unfortunately that is a limitation from After Effects, not a limitation from Cyclops. You will need to render compositions individually.

### Cyclops is not rendering paths on shape layers, masks on solids, roto shapes from mocha, or any other path when multiple layers are selected. Why?

Unfortunately that is a limitation from After Effects, not a limitation from Cyclops. I’ve tried to come up with a good solution and I haven’t been able to yet. Let me know if you have an idea!

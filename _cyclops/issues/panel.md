# Panel Won’t Open

If you’re experiencing an issue where Cyclops won’t open when clicking **Window** > **Extensions** > **Cyclops**, you may need to use this solution:

1. Open the [Manager App](https://aescripts.com/learn/aescripts-aeplugins-manager-app/) or the [ZXP Installer](https://aescripts.com/learn/zxp-installer/) from [aescripts + aeplugins](https://aescripts.com/).
2. Click the gear icon to open settings.
3. Click the ZXP icon to open CEP settings.
4. Ensure that every checkbox labeled **Enable debugging** is checked.
5. Click the **OK** button.
6. Restart After Effects and try Cyclops again.

{: .important }
> This issue is caused by the aescripts + aeplugins licensing framework. Learn more on the official [aescripts + aeplugins](https://aescripts.com/knowledgebase/index/view/faq/zxp-cep-extension-won-t-open/) help page.
